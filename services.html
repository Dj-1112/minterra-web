Our Capabilities

Craft at Every Stage

Every service we offer is delivered by the same team, under the same roof — so nothing gets lost between design and delivery.

🏛️

Architecture & Design

From concept to construction documentation, we develop architectural solutions that are as structurally sound as they are beautiful — resolving every detail before a single brick is laid.

  • Concept design & 3D visualisation
  • Working drawings & technical documentation
  • BBMP / municipal plan approvals
  • Structural coordination
🔨

Construction & Build

End-to-end construction management with skilled labour, vetted subcontractors, quality materials, and real-time updates — so you always know where your project stands.

  • Site management & supervision
  • Subcontractor coordination
  • Material procurement & quality control
  • Timeline & budget tracking
🪑

Interior Design

Spaces that are designed from the inside out — where materiality, light, and furniture work together to make a building feel like home or a workplace people want to be in.

  • Space planning & layout
  • Material & finish selection
  • Furniture specification
  • Lighting design
🔄

Renovation & Retrofit

Transforming existing structures into something better — structurally, functionally, and aesthetically — while respecting what makes the original space worth keeping.

  • Structural assessment & planning
  • Phased renovation management
  • Heritage restoration (where applicable)
  • MEP upgrades & modernisation
📋

Project Management

For clients who already have a design team but need construction oversight — we act as your on-site eyes, managing quality, timeline, and contractor accountability so you don't have to.

  • Independent site supervision
  • Weekly progress reporting
  • Contractor evaluation & selection
  • Defect identification & resolution
🌿

Landscaping

The space outside the building matters as much as the space inside. We design outdoor areas that are purposeful, sustainable, and beautiful — from private gardens to commercial exteriors.

  • Garden & courtyard design
  • Hardscape & softscape planning
  • Irrigation & drainage
  • Native planting for sustainability
Our Process

How We Work

A clear, four-stage process from first conversation to final handover.

1

Discovery

We listen first. We understand your vision, your budget, your timeline, and your non-negotiables before a single line is drawn.

2

Design

Concepts, drawings, 3D visualisations, and approvals — fully resolved before construction begins, so there are no surprises.

3

Build

Site work begins with weekly updates and open communication. We manage every contractor, material, and milestone on your behalf.

4

Handover

Snag list cleared, documentation handed over, and a final walkthrough with you to confirm every detail meets the design intent.
